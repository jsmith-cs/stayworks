About Stayworks

Transforming Property Management Through Innovation

Our Mission

To revolutionize property management by providing innovative, user-friendly solutions that empower landlords and property managers to efficiently manage their properties while delivering exceptional experiences to their tenants.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a group of students from McMaster University, Stayworks emerged from a simple observation: property management shouldn't be complicated. Our team of industry experts and tech innovators came together with a shared vision of creating a platform that simplifies the complexities of property management.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of property managers and landlords across Canada, helping them streamline their operations and grow their businesses with the help of Stayworks.
